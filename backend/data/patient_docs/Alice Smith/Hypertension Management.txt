Patient Care Document: Alice Smith (Hypertension Management)

Daily Guidelines:
1. Medication: Take Lisinopril 10mg once daily in the morning with a full glass of water. Take Hydrochlorothiazide 25mg once daily at lunchtime.
2. Blood Pressure Monitoring: Check blood pressure twice daily—once in the morning and once in the evening. Record all readings.
3. Diet: Maintain a low-sodium diet (under 1,500 mg of sodium per day). Avoid processed foods, canned soups, and pickles.
4. Warnings: Call the office immediately if your systolic blood pressure is over 160 mmHg or if your diastolic blood pressure is over 100 mmHg.
5. In case of mild dizziness when standing up, sit back down immediately and rest.
